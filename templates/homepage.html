WELCOME TO SSN HELPDESK

Welcome to the Campus Helpdesk, your one-stop destination for resolving all campus-related issues promptly and efficiently. From WiFi connectivity to electrical glitches, plumbing emergencies, and more, we're here to ensure a seamless experience for all members of our campus community.

STEPS FOR LOGGING A PROBLEM:
